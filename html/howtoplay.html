How to Play – Lumora

Welcome to your space. There is no rush here.

  1. Choose an item Pick an item from the panel on the left.
  2. Place it on the island Drag it into the island area to shape your world.
  3. Start a gentle task Begin a task whenever you feel ready — at your own pace.
  4. You are not alone The emergency button is always available if you need support.

Nothing here is timed. Nothing is judged. This space is yours.
