Emergency & Crisis Support

Lumora is a self-help game. It cannot replace professional support or emergency services. If you are in acute distress, feel unsafe, or think you might hurt yourself or someone else, please reach out for help immediately.

1. If you are in immediate danger (Austria)

Call one of these emergency numbers right away:

  • 112 – European emergency number (works EU-wide)
  • 144 – Ambulance / medical emergency
  • 133 – Police
  • 122 – Fire brigade
  • 140 – Mountain rescue
  • 141 – Medical on-call service
  • 01 406 43 43 – Poison information centre

If you are not sure which number is right, call 112 or 144.

2. Emotional crisis – talking to someone (Austria)

If you feel overwhelmed, hopeless, or alone, you deserve support. You can reach out to:

  • Telefonseelsorge – 142
    24/7, free of charge, confidential. For anyone in emotional distress.
  • Rat auf Draht – 147
    24/7 helpline for children and young people.
  • Women’s Helpline – 0800 222 555
    Support for women affected by violence or in threatening situations.
  • Victim emergency call – 0800 112 112
    Support if you have experienced violence or crime.

Many of these services also offer online chat or email counselling. Check their websites for more information and language options.

3. Outside Austria or travelling

If you are not in Austria, please contact the emergency and crisis services in the country you are in.

Emergency numbers in many countries include for example:

  • 112 – in most European countries
  • 911 – United States & Canada
  • 999 – United Kingdom & Ireland
  • 000 – Australia
  • 111 – New Zealand

For emotional crisis support worldwide, you can search for helplines in your country via:

  • findahelpline.com – global directory of crisis and emotional support lines
  • befrienders.org – worldwide network of suicide prevention helplines

4. Keeping yourself safe in this moment

  • Stay in a place where you feel as safe as possible.
  • Avoid alcohol or drugs if you can.
  • Reach out to someone you trust (friend, family member, partner).
  • Write down the numbers above or save them in your phone.

You do not have to get through this alone.
Reaching out is a strong and courageous step.
